About Section

As part of my thesis project, I'm developing a series of educational and enriching mobile games for kids. This game utilizes basic physics and touch recognition in the style of Flappy Bird to provide a game platform that is familiar with a large demographic. In combining simple game mechanics with math, I hope to produce a fun way to practice math in a gamified and fulfilling way.

Aside from basic math, I also plan on creating different levels of difficulty to cater to a wider audience. For example, K-1 will have a level tailored to their needs, as will grades 5-6. I want to create diverse math game that can be accessible to more than one age group. Therefore, styling of the game (artwork and sounds) must be thoughout to adhere to many ages.
